# CNC Wishlist

Parts that need machining. Designed for multi-axis CNC with auto tool change.

**Reference:** See [volvo-740-subframe-standards.md](../../volvo-740-subframe-standards.md) for subframe requirements and best practices.

**CNC Parts List:**
- Front subframe (Steel): Custom, reinforced, Saab B204/B234 + M90 fitment
- Förstärkningsplåtar bakaxel (Steel): Rear axle reinforcement plates
- Motorfästen (Billet aluminium): Engine mounts
- Mellanväggar (Steel): Dividing wall, doubles as roll cage base
- Emergency exit mechanism (Steel/Aluminium): Mechanical, no electronics, ratchet, 1-ton stored force

All parts must fit stock exterior dimensions. Nothing visible from outside.
