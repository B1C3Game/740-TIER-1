# Tier 1 Build: Subframe, Engine Swap, and Tuning

## Base Vehicle
- **Model:** Volvo 740
- **Year Range:** 1985–1992 (typical donor)
- **Chassis:** Rear-wheel drive (RWD), sedan or wagon
- **Condition:** Stock exterior, minimal rust, solid frame preferred
- **Original Engine:** Redblock 4-cylinder (B230F or similar)

## Tier 1 Focus
Tier 1 is a practical, daily-driveable upgrade path focused on reliability and foundational performance. The scope is limited to the following areas:



### 1. Subframe & Frame Enhancements
- Custom front subframe designed for improved strength, handling, and precise fitment of the Saab B204/B234 turbo engine and Volvo M90 gearbox
- Rear axle reinforcement plates (Förstärkningsplåtar bakaxel) for added durability under load
- Dividing wall (mellanväggar) doubles as roll cage base for increased chassis rigidity and safety
- All frame enhancements are designed for CNC manufacturing (see [CNC Wishlist](../cnc/wishlist.md))
- Follows standards and best practices as detailed in [volvo-740-subframe-standards.md](../volvo-740-subframe-standards.md)
- Retains compatibility with stock suspension geometry

### 2. Engine Swap (Specified)
- Swap in a Saab B204 or B234 turbo engine, paired with a Volvo M90 gearbox
- Follow the detailed process and tuning recommendations in [volvo-740-saab-turbo-m90-towing-tuning.md](../volvo-740-saab-turbo-m90-towing-tuning.md)
- Ensure all mounts, wiring, and plumbing are reliable and serviceable
- Maintain emissions compliance where required

### 3. Tuning
- Custom ECU remap for high low-end torque and towing capability
- Use high-flow injectors, upgraded fuel pump, and wideband O2 sensor
- Turbo selection and supporting mods as detailed in the tuning guide
- Focus on smooth drivability, reliability, and safe power gains


## Price Estimates: Lowest Viable Tier 1

**Lowest viable build includes:**
- 740 shell (no engine)
- Saab B204/B234 turbo engine (used)
- Volvo M90 gearbox (used)
- Custom subframe swap

**Estimated Costs:**
- 740 shell (no engine): ~15k SEK
- Saab B204/B234 turbo (used): ~3–8k SEK (common on Blocket/breakers)
- Volvo M90 gearbox (used): ~2–5k SEK
- Subframe steel (materials): ~2–5k SEK (estimate, need CNC shop to confirm)
- Misc (mounts, bolts, adapters, fluids): ~2–3k SEK

**Lowest viable total:** ~25–35k SEK

Tier 1 is designed to be accessible and reversible, with all modifications compatible with a stock-appearing Volvo 740. The goal is a car that feels OEM+ in daily use, with a strong foundation for future upgrades.
